Rules for the Spaghetti Bridge Project

You can only use 250g of spaghettis and 5 glue sticks. Your score is calculated by Mass Held By Bridge (g)/Mass of Bridge (kg). Your Bridge can only be 10cm in width, 60cm in lenght, and 25cm in height.

Objective is to make a spaghetti bridge that can hold as much weight as it could possibly hold.
